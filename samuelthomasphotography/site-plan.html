Overview

Purpose

This website is to display and present my photography skills and services. It will include examples, pricing, location, and scheduling photoshoots.

Audience

My audience will include the following:

  1. Students: those seeking graduation photos, profile photos or group photos. Likely age 19-25, tight income, and hoping for a handful of really good pictures to use.
  2. Couples: those seeking engagement photos or wedding photos. Likely age 19-25, tight income, hoping for lots of pictures to choose from, etc.
  3. Businesses: those seeking stock photos, event photos, or standardized employee photos. Likely leaders, professional, and timely.

About and Location

About Me When starting high school my grandma kindly offered her broken camera for my birthday if I simply repaired it. Since then I have shared my journeys and thank her through the photographs she blessed me with the tool to seek! While I have always had a love of nature and light photography, I have always strived for the opportunity to deepen my skills with photographing people and those around me - the best part of our lives. I took photography classes through high school where I found the opportunity to see the sights we often miss as I roamed the campus looking for new photos in each hallway and window. This is where I mastered adjusting my camera for the perfect outcome as I learned to use the tools of Lightroom and Photoshop. All throughout my missionary service from 2021-2023 I took the opportunities that I could to photograph along the way. Here I really got to sharpen my skills with photographing people. Since being home and now studying in Rexburg, Idaho at BYUI for a minor in photography, I have photographed any adventure along the way. And I'm ready to photograph how you would like to be seen, and what you would like to always remember. Locations:
I will happily travel up to 2.5 hours to meet for a photoshoot. If over an hour away there is a $10 travel fee. I will be at the following locations throughout the year:
January 10th - July 15th: Rexburg, Idaho and surrounding areas
July 20th - September 12th: Cumming, Georgia and surrounding areas
September 15th - December 5th: Rexburg, Idaho and surrounding areas
December 8th - January 8th: Cumming, Goergia and surrounding areas
